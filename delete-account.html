Delete Your SETU Manager Account

⚠️ Warning: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your business data, customer records, invoices, and transaction history will be permanently deleted.

What data will be deleted?

  • Your account (mobile number and password)
  • All customer records
  • All invoices and transactions
  • All work entries and business data
  • All marketing poster data
  • Notification settings and preferences

What data will be retained?

  • No data is retained after account deletion. All data is permanently removed within 30 days.

How to request account deletion

Option 1 — Email Request (Recommended):
  1. Send an email to setumanagers@gmail.com
  2. Subject: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include your registered mobile number in the email
  4. We will delete your account within 7 business days
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once deleted
