Privacy Policy – SETU Manager

Last updated: June 23, 2026

SETU Manager ("we", "our", or "us") is a business management application designed for small businesses and VLE (Village Level Entrepreneurs) in India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the SETU Manager Android application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and mobile number used during registration and login.
  • Business Data: Customer records, invoices, transactions, and work entries that you enter into the app.
  • Device Information: Device model, Android version, and app version for technical support purposes.
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns to improve the application experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the SETU Manager service.
  • To store and sync your business data securely via Firebase (Google Cloud).
  • To send daily business reminder notifications (with your permission).
  • To verify your subscription/license status.
  • To respond to your support requests.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase Firestore, which is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

4. Permissions Used

  • Internet: Required to sync your business data with the cloud.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): Used to send daily business reminders (7am, 9am, 10am, 3pm, 5pm). You can disable these at any time from your device settings.
  • Storage (WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – Android 9 and below only): Used only when you choose to save marketing poster images to your device gallery. This permission is requested before saving any image.
  • Network State: To check internet connectivity and show appropriate messages.
  • Receive Boot Completed: To reschedule your daily reminders after device restart.

5. Marketing Posters and Gallery

SETU Manager allows you to create and save marketing posters to your device gallery. Images are saved only when you explicitly tap the "Save" button. On Android 10 and above, no storage permission is required. On Android 9 and below, the app will request your permission before saving any image.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database, and cloud storage. (Firebase Privacy Policy)
  • WhatsApp: Used only when you choose to share content via WhatsApp. No data is shared automatically.

7. Data Retention

Your business data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email address below.

8. Children's Privacy

SETU Manager is designed for business use by adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
